Mathematics 12

This is the final mathematics course for many high school students in Nova Scotia. For those continuing on to calculus, there will be some concepts carried over, as there was in math 11. The overarching units in the course are as follows,

  • Quadratics
  • Rate of Change
  • Exponential Growth
  • Circle Geometry
  • Probability

Exponential Growth

This unit explores exponential equations. We study the growth and properties of the equations and how it translates to the graphs. We also learn how to use logarithms and bases to solve complex exponential equations.
