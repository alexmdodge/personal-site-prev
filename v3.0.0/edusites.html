  • Kahn Academy

    This site all started from one man's desire to help tutor his nephew. It has grown into a multifaceted teaching tool which provides support resources for both students and teachers.

  • Hyperphysics

    A site started by a professor at Georgia State University, it is a comprehensive mind map of every topic in physics. The site has internal applications as well as a standalone application. It contains images, diagrams, and other useful physics related content, all devoted to helping teachers convey material.

  • Wolfram Alpha

    A side project of Mathmatica, this online computation tool has turned into a huge modelling application from which any topic can be searched and analysed. Along with solving complex math, Wolfram offers the ability to search historical dates, model trends, and look deeper into the meaning of, well, anything.

  • ipl2

    This is an online database which until recently I had not known existed. Search anything in the bar and it will link a series of articles, papers, as well as websites which are not typically on Google's funded list.

  • Scholastic

    Everyone has read a Scholastic book at some point or another, but that's not all they do. On this site are resources for students, parents, and even curriculum based material for teachers to access.

  • TED Ed

    With the success of TED talks, many of the speakers got together with professional artists to develop creative videos which could be used in classrooms. This website offers teachers the abilities to create lessons, add videos, and their own notes, and even contribute their own videos to the website.

  • We Are Teachers

    An incredibly detailed and rich site. From primary to grade 12, this site has resources, lesson plans, tips, and so much more. It also has blogs and external resources and in general is an extremely useful site.

  • Discovery Ed

    An incredibly useful resource on its own, Discovery has collected a vast number of resources for teachers in terms of lesson plans, videos, and other content which is easily accessible and ready for the classroom.
