Physics 12

This class builds heavily on the skills and concepts developed in Physics 11. The main extension is a larger section of modern physics, and all of the previous skills will now be dealt with in two dimensions. The overarching units in the course are as follows,

  • Force, Motion, Work, and Energy
  • Fields
  • Waves and Modern Physics
  • Radioactivity

hello

Waves and Modern Physics

This unit begins with Electrostatics and ends with Magnetism. It's important to begin by relating the effects of gravity with that of charged particles. Students will have already worked with problems relating to gravitational fields, and these units simply expand on those ideas.
